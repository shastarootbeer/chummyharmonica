Poems by Billy F. Sleazo

I Yearn

Everyday I awake
To the same old same
I yearn for a change
For Christ's sake
Something good or something strange
I yearn for it everyday

Some Year

If not this year then some year
Some year, I will find happiness
Even if it kills me
If not this year some year
Some year, I'll be content
Even if I'm an addict

Watching Through Eyes I Have No Possession Of

I'm watching through eyes I have no possession of
I am not my body
My body is not mine
I'm just a soul
Stuck here until I'm released
By the grace of God
I'm stuck in this hell vessel
I feel like a demon sent into a pig by God
Watching through eyes I have no possession of
